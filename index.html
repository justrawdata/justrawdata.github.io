About Caritas

caritas [ kah-ri-tahs; English kar-i-tas ]
noun Latin.
1. charity.
Caritas is a project started by a solo artist and developer, Maxwell Watts aimed at creating unique, collectible NFT series that serve a good cause. Each series of NFTs will be used to raise funds to donate to different organizations. Each series will all have their own unique set of perks and a roadmap specific to it.
